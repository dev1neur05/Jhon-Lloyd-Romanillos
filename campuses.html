Our Campuses

The University of Mindanao operates across multiple campuses in the Davao region, providing accessible education and state-of-the-art facilities to students.

Main Campuses

  • Matina Campus - Located in Davao City, home to the main administrative offices and several colleges.
  • Bolton Campus - Offers specialized programs in business and technology.
  • Bangkal Campus - Focuses on community outreach and vocational training.
  • Other Satellite Campuses - Including locations in nearby areas for expanded reach.

Each campus is equipped with modern classrooms, libraries, and recreational facilities to support student success.
